About Tailwaggers

Tailwaggers is a passionate, community-driven animal shelter dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming pets in need. Our mission is to create a world where every animal has a loving home and a second chance at happiness.

Since our founding, we have helped hundreds of dogs and cats find their forever families. Our team of volunteers and supporters work tirelessly to provide medical care, foster homes, and adoption services for animals who deserve a better life.

Join us in making a difference—whether you adopt, volunteer, or donate, you’re helping us save lives and spread love, one paw at a time.
