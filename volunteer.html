About the experience

Operated by a small team of full-time staff, our volunteers are absolutely critical to our existence. They help us to meet the physical demands of day-to-day operations and ensure that we give every dog and cat sufficient medical care and love.

Our volunteer program also serves as a supplementary source of revenue as a means of funding beyond donations. Simply put, volunteers are what keep our doors open!

Through our volunteer program, animal lovers are offered an opportunity of experiencing first-hand the joys and challenges of working at an animal rescue organization, as well as learning what it takes to run an organization like this.

Why us?

Animal volunteer opportunities in Thailand are plentiful, but we will provide you with unmatched, hands-on experience. You will leave Rescue P.A.W.S. feeling accomplished, having experienced both the exciting but, also trying elements of stray animal care in Thailand. You will also get the opportunity to make friends from all over the world, build long-lasting relationships and become part of our close-knit family!
